Research

My research sits at the intersection of computer vision, medical imaging, and generative AI — building systems that understand, segment, and reconstruct clinical data.

Medical Image Segmentation with Quantum-Inspired Edge Enhancement

Dense pixel-level breast tissue segmentation using quantum-inspired Gabor edge enhancement and UNet++ with EfficientNet. Surpassed state-of-the-art in a challenging low-data regime.

Active Research

2D-to-3D Volumetric Reconstruction from Clinical Imaging

Building a pipeline that lifts 2D segmentation predictions into volumetric 3D representations, enabling dense spatial understanding from stacked multi-slice imaging data (L1–L5 vertebrae through full spinal column).

3D CNNs Multi-slice fusion MONAI

Code Reasoning Shorthand: Dense LLM Tokenization with DPO Alignment

Designing a novel dense notation system for LLM reasoning that reduces generation tokens by 60–75% while preserving semantic information. Fine-tuning 8B-parameter model with DPO alignment and adaptive generation depth.

60-75% token reduction 3-4× speedup 8B params
